### BitBoard representation 
```bash
8 | . . . . . . . .
7 | . . . . . . . .
6 | . . . . . . . .
5 | . . . . . . . .
4 | . . . . . . . .
3 | . . . . . . . .
2 | . . . . . . . .
1 | . . . . . . . .
    - - - - - - - - 
    h g f e d c b a
```

The board is flipped about the Positive y axis making it a little confusing for visualizing left and right of a piece . This is known as `Little-endian rank-file mapping(LERF)` .
